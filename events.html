The problem

A task that never should have been manual

At Quantistry, staying on top of industry conferences meant checking dozens of websites, subscribing to newsletters, and waiting for event dates and deadlines to surface — only to repeat the process the following month.

Pain points

  • —Checking 20+ websites manually every month for new event announcements
  • —Missing early-bird deadlines because information surfaced too late
  • —No centralised view of upcoming events, dates, and submission deadlines
  • —Time spent on research that could be spent on actual marketing work

Seven search queries run in parallel via Tavily's search API, returning up to 35 results. Claude reads each result and extracts structured event data — name, date, location, deadline, and relevance score — filtering out anything that isn't a real event. Valid events are automatically appended to a Google Sheet.
